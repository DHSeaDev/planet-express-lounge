⚡ Setup — two steps
1
Get a free API key
Groq is recommended — free, fast, no credit card needed. OpenRouter and Google Gemini are also supported.
→ console.groq.com (recommended) → openrouter.ai/keys → aistudio.google.com
2
Paste your key in Settings
Open the sidebar → ⚙️ Settings → select provider, paste your key, press 💾 Save & Connect. The status dot turns green. Demo Mode exits. Full access enabled.
Optional — Premium expressive voices: by default, character voices use your operating system's built-in speech engine (or Chrome's, as a fallback). For higher-quality, more expressive voices across all 18 characters, add an ElevenLabs key in ⚙️ Settings → 🎙️ Voice Engine → 💎 ElevenLabs. Sensible default voices are pre-assigned to all 6 voice categories, and you can swap in your own favorites from the ElevenLabs Voice Library any time. Note: ElevenLabs' free tier does not include API access for text-to-speech — a paid ElevenLabs plan is required for this to work. Without one, the extension automatically falls back to OS/Chrome voices. This is a separate, optional premium key billed to your own ElevenLabs account. Not required for any other feature.

📡 Features
🎬
Demo Mode
No API key? No problem. Demo Mode activates automatically — scripted crew exchanges with full TTS voices, and a complete pre-written autopilot episode. Exits permanently when you connect a key.
💬
Ensemble Chat
Send a message — the best-matched crew member responds instantly. LLM router + keyword matching picks the right voice every time.
🤖
Autopilot
The crew picks its own topic and debates through a full sitcom episode arc — cold open to button joke — hands free. Pause, resume, save, and summarise.
🧪
Professor's Lab
Generate an LLM-powered invention, send it to the crew, recycle scraps into Dark Matter, and unlock four Dark Matter Widgets below.
🎙️
Character Voices
OS voices (Windows/macOS) prioritised for richer audio. 18 distinct pitch and rate profiles. Master volume, per-character mutes, and Chrome voice override toggle.
📌
Cold Storage
Pin any message, save AI summaries, or store full transcripts. Export any saved item as a printable document, or share it to Reddit, Facebook, X, or Email. Stored entirely in your local browser IndexedDB.
💀
Chaos Mode
TV-appropriate profanity, maximum fourth-wall breakage, and inter-character insults. Use responsibly.
⚛️ Dark Matter Widgets

Four mini-games live in the Lab, spending the same Dark Matter pool as the Invention system. Earn Dark Matter passively: 1–3 ⚛️ every 5 seconds while exploring tabs, 5–11 ⚛️ every 5 seconds while Autopilot runs, 1 ⚛️ per chat message you send, plus the usual invention scrapping & recycling rewards.

📡
Aurebesh Relay
Decrypt the alien glyph network in three sequential tiers, each unlocking the next: view the Aurebesh archive (50 ⚛️), then activate a live English ↔ Aurebesh translator (100 ⚛️), then breach the frequency to intercept encrypted crew chatter (50 ⚛️).
📜
Expedition Log
Break the wax seal (75 ⚛️) on the Captain's journal, then log unlimited AI-generated expedition reports from the crew's deliveries.
👃
Smell-O-Scope™
Sniff for lore (50 ⚛️ per scan) — pulls a random Futurama fact, easter egg, or trivia entry, plus an in-character crew reaction. Builds your Lab Notes collection.
🥤
Beverage Machine
Dispense a Slurm can (25 ⚛️ per spin). Weighted rarity — collect 8 flavours, from Original to the secret 1%-drop Milk. ♻️ Recycle extras for Dark Matter (5–250 ⚛️ by rarity), with a ×N counter per flavour.
